Conceptual Inspiration

Spinoza

Humans & the peculiarities of humans not separate from Nature--infinite & eternal substance--but finite modes of this substance

Nature/God without purpose, end goals

Human ideas & actions are attributes of Nature, and have as much free will as natural events.

Striving to persevere--increase power of our acting of our acting is freedom

Life led by passions(senses & imagination)--changable, external--is bondage

Present Mass Extinction Event

Human causes--from belief in existance separate from Nature? from being led by passions?

Cancer--malfunctioning, growing & destroying environment, not the same as persevering as the outcome is self-destruction

Is an extinction the end of an attribute of Nature?

Meta

Destruction of an environment that supports humans != destruction of Nature

Matter will not be destroyed

Nature persisted through previous mass extinctions

Control/communication

Cordyceps spores growing in host body, bursting through
