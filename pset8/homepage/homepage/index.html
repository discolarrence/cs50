Notes for Unfurling of Desire Project

By Laura Bernethia

Introduction

The Education of Desire

A show at Kore Gallery with paintings by Sabra Crockett featuring wearable art by Edwin Ramirez. The show explores humans' relationship with nature and the value they place on it. Subject matter is largely endangered/extinct species native to the surrounding region.

Unfurling of Desire

An event that serves as an afterparty for the opening of The Education of Desire. Installations/decor at the event can connect to the themes of the art show and/or the concept of unfurling itself.

The Setting

The event will be 9PM-3AM 11/6/2021 in the event space at Art Sanctuary. House/dance music all night.

My Project

I want to incorporate some fiberarts techniques I've been practicing to explore various concepts & conversations I've been thinking about. I don't have the exact final look, but definitely some visual inspiration.
